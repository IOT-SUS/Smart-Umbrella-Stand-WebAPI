Product

We expects to invent an IoT device in the umbrella stand, which can record where and when the user put the umbrella. If the owner does not retrieve his umbrella after a while, then our Smart Umbrella Stands will send a message telling the owner that you may have forgotten that you took your umbrella back. In addition, we also provide a courtesy umbrella service. If the owner does not get his umbrella back within a predetermined period of time, our system will turn it into a courtesy umbrella for users.
